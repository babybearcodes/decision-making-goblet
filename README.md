# decision-making-goblet
End your decision-making paralysis. Let the goblet choose your next task :)
## How to Use

1. Click the small parchment
2. Write your tasks one by one
3. Press Enter after each task
4. Double-click the parchment to roll it
5. Drag it into the goblet fire
6. Voila..the goblet reveals your chosen task 🐻
